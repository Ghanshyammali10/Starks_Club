STARKS TECH CLUB

Building the Next Generation of Innovators

STARKS is the official technology club of SVKM's College of Engineering, Shirpur. We bring together passionate students from CSE, IT, ECE, DS and all engineering branches to collaborate, build, and grow through hands-on technical experiences.

VISION

A campus where every student who wants to build — can build.

To create a strong technical community within SVKM Shirpur where students collaborate, share knowledge, and develop real-world skills that prepare them for industry challenges.

MISSION

  • Encourage students to learn modern technologies and development tools
  • Promote collaborative learning and peer-to-peer mentorship
  • Encourage participation in hackathons, coding competitions, and technical events
  • Support students in building strong GitHub portfolios and LinkedIn profiles
  • Help students develop real-world projects that showcase their skills
